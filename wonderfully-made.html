Dedication Example

For Cheyenne Suzette Grimes,

My brave girl.

It was the greatest honor of my life to stand beside you while you fought your way into strength.

I watched you endure more before six months old than most do in a lifetime. I watched you learn to walk with a pole beside you. I watched you smile through scars.

And I would do it all again in a heartbeat.

Every surgery.
Every long night.
Every prayer whispered over your hospital bed.

Because you are worth everything.

God has been so good to us.

Love,
Mom

Prayer Page Example

Lord,

Thank You for children who fight with quiet courage.
Thank You for doctors, nurses, and miracles we cannot see.

For every parent reading this who is walking hospital halls, give them strength.

For every child with tubes, scars, or a body that works differently, cover them in peace.

Help them trust You.
Help them be still and know that You are God.

Remind them they are fearfully and wonderfully made.

Give them courage for today
and hope for tomorrow.

Amen.
