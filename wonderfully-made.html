Wonderfully Made: Special Stories

Custom faith-based children's books created for brave kids with medical journeys, rare diagnoses, and unique challenges.

Each Custom Book: $20

Why This Exists

Some children begin life in hospitals. Some grow up with scars, feeding tubes, or bodies that work differently. Their stories deserve to be told gently, truthfully, and with hope.

Inspired by my daughter Cheyenne, a survivor of Short Gut Syndrome, this collection honors courage, resilience, and the truth that every child is fearfully and wonderfully made.
