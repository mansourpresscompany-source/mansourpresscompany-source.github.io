Example title: Cheyenne’s Brave Belly

Our Story

Cheyenne was born with Short Gut Syndrome.

Short Gut Syndrome (also called Short Bowel Syndrome) is a rare condition where a child’s body does not have enough small intestine to absorb the nutrients it needs. For many families, that means feeding tubes, IV nutrition, surgeries, and long stretches inside hospital walls.

That was our story.

Before she was six months old, we had already faced more than most families do in a lifetime. We learned medical terms we never wanted to know. We counted ounces. We watched monitors. We prayed through the night.

And we watched God carry us.

This book is not a marketing example. It is written in honor of my daughter’s strength and in testimony to God’s goodness in our hardest season.

Wonderfully Made was born from that journey — so that other parents walking hospital halls can place a story in their child’s hands that speaks courage, identity, and truth.

No diagnosis defines a child. And every child is fearfully and wonderfully made.

How Custom Books Work
