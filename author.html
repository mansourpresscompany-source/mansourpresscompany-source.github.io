About the Author

S.C. Mansour

S.C. Mansour holds a Bachelor’s degree in English Literature and brings her love of language and deep Christian faith into every story she writes.

She is also the illustrator behind Mansour Press—creating warm, storybook worlds that invite children to wonder, rest, and remember God’s love.
